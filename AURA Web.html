we are

AURA is a company that specializes in building strong brands and creating impactful spaces for businesses of all sizes, from startups to large corporations. Our goal is to bridge the gap between businesses and customers by creating an emotional attachment that’s often missing in many businesses.

In addition to branding, we offer comprehensive interior design services, transforming commercial, office, and residential spaces into functional and visually striking environments. We also provide a unique and specialized service in "Praying Room Interior Design" for Orthodox religion followers, catering to both practical and spiritual needs.

As a legally registered Private Limited Company (PLC) under the Ethiopian Commercial Code, we have the expertise and experience to help your business succeed. We understand that both strong branding and exceptional interior design are essential for success in today's uncertain times. Our team of professionals will work with you to create a brand and space that will capture people's attention, allowing your business to stand out from the competition.

Investing in a strong brand and remarkable spaces with Aura will ensure your business has the best chance of success in the future.
